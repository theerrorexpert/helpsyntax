NAME
       connectparticipant -

DESCRIPTION
       Amazon  Connect  is a cloud-based contact center solution that makes it
       easy to set up and manage a customer contact center and  provide  reli-
       able customer engagement at any scale.

       Amazon Connect enables customer contacts through voice or chat.

       The  APIs  described here are used by chat participants, such as agents
       and customers.

AVAILABLE COMMANDS

       o create-participant-connection

       o disconnect-participant

       o get-attachment

       o get-transcript

       o help

       o send-event

       o send-message
